Positive Contributions made by Computer Science to Society

  • Computing in wartime

  • Alan Turing along with his allies at Bletchley Park in Buckinhamshire made efforts to break the infamous German Enigma code. This resulted in the creation of the bombe machine. This allowed the code-breakers to read German Air Force signals and assisted with the war effort.

  • Databases

  • Improvments in technology has phased out archaic methods of storing information. Instead of recording a list on paper, data can now be stored in a database which means data can be stored in a mannner that is more orderly and easier to access.

  • Communication

  • With the advent of communication services such as SMS, email, or the numerous communication platforms such as Whatsapp, Line, or Messenger, contacting loved ones has never been easier. No matter how far a loved one is from you, you will always be connected with them.
